How Can the Vacant Tax Law Help Us?

With the vacant tax law, the supply of housing units can be increased by giving individuals an incentive to put their unit on the rental market, as well as provide revenue that can be used to fund more construction of residential units. It has worked in cities such as Vancouver, where “data has shown an increase in the supply of rental properties,” and a ”total of $40 million generated in 2018.”

With the generated revenue from the vacant tax law, we can also fund inclusionary zoning (have units targeted towards specific income classes) to further provide affordable housing in Toronto. The vacant tax will ensure that all units will be used to optimize the number of units that are avaiable for everyone. All units will contribute to the affordability crisis recovery in one way or another.
